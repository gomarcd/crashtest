Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 18, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Crashtest ("we", "our", or "the application") handles information when you use our application. Crashtest is designed with privacy in mind and operates on the principle of minimal data collection.

Information Collection and Use

Crashtest is a standalone API client application that:

  • Does not collect personal information from users
  • Does not require account creation or user registration
  • Does not use cookies or persistent identifiers
  • Does not track your usage or activity
  • Does not store your API requests on our servers
  • Does not transmit data to our servers or third parties

All API requests you make in the application are sent directly to the endpoints you specify. Any data generated during the use of the application (such as API responses) remains on your local device and is not transmitted to us.

Data Storage

Crashtest:

  • Operates locally on your device
  • Does not use cloud storage for your data
  • Does not maintain databases of user information
  • Does not create user profiles or save usage history
  • Does not persist data between sessions unless explicitly saved by you

Data Security

Since Crashtest does not collect, store, or transmit personal data, there is minimal risk to your personal information. The application is designed to work entirely on your local device.

Third-Party Services

When you use Crashtest to connect to third-party APIs:

  • Your requests are sent directly to those third-party services
  • Any data shared with those services is governed by their respective privacy policies
  • We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party services

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there are no specific actions required to exercise your privacy rights regarding data we hold. Your data remains entirely under your control.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Compliance Statement

This privacy policy has been prepared to meet requirements of various privacy regulations including GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable laws, even though our application does not collect personal data that would typically be subject to these regulations.
